Computer Architecture and Systems Lab

Computer Architecture and Systems Laboratory (CASys) was established in 1974 when the KAIST CS department was founded. Along the long history, the lab has been exploring a wide spectrum of research topics in computer architecture and systems. Building upon the profound research tradition, the faculty and student members of CASys currently work on addressing interdisciplinary research challenges that span across a broad range of layers in computing stack, which include but not limited to, (1) systems for machine learning (ML), (2) processor and memory system design for edge-to-cloud systems, (3) operating system support for emerging memory technologies, and (4) trusted computing for system security.
